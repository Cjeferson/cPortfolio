NMV SUMMER LEAGUE

As a volunteer content creator for our community basketball league, I honed my Adobe Photoshop and Canva skills while designing engaging social media posters. This hands-on experience enhanced my design capabilities and understanding of sports-focused content creation.

IDOL BASKETBALL

I created social media content to promote the launch of this new basketball league, including game schedules, player highlights, and weekly features such as Player of the Game and Player of the Week. In addition to digital content, I designed the official league jerseys inspired by NBA aesthetics. Instead of team names, I incorporated the league’s branding — “IDOL” — to establish a strong and cohesive visual identity that reflects professionalism while maintaining the league’s unique character.
