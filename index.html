About

Graduate of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Major in Network Technology with a passion for blending technology and creativity. I love building digital solutions that are not only functional but also meaningful and user-friendly.

From developing IoT-powered systems and cloud-integrated apps to designing intuitive interfaces and creative graphics, I enjoy turning ideas into real, impactful projects. My capstone project, MeasuringBot, is a great example — transforming a traditional measuring wheel into a smart, cloud-connected tool.

When I’m not coding or designing, I’m exploring new tools, learning fresh skills, and finding ways to make technology work smarter.
